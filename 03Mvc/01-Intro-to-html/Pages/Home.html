Article to make coffee

for 2 people

Ingredients

We need 4 ingredients:
  • Water - 2 cups
  • ground coffee - 4 tbsp
    • prefer to use medium roast columbian beans
  • sugar
    1. white
    2. brown
    3. Splenda
  • milk/creamer - as per taste/optional

Method

Boil/brew 2 cups of water in a pot. Add 4 tbsp of ground coffee powder. Boil it for 3 mins or brew it for 5 mins in the in the coffee pot. Add sugar or milk as per taste

Serve coffee

Serve the coffee in the mug (prefer to add hot milk for winters)
